Buttons

This is the list of buttons and what each one does with respect to the turtle and the canvas.

  • Dance Button

    • Clicking the Dance button will make the turtle dance in it's current location, first by spinning clockwise, then anti-clockwise, moving side-to-side, then up and down, and finally a last clockwise spin.

  • Sleep Button

    • Clicking the Sleep button will make the turtle close its eyes then fall asleep, with z's appearing above the turtle's head whilst he sleeps.

  • Walk Button

    • Clicking the Walk button makes the turtle walk in a square of length and width 200px with the centre of the square in the turtle's current position at the time of clicking.

  • Reset Button

    • Clicking the Reset button will reset all of the canvas' assets to the original positions.

Clicking the Canvas

This is the list of different actions when clicking various parts of the canvas and what each does with respect to the turtle and the canvas.

  • Click on Turtle Shell

    • Clicking the turtle's shell will make the turtle go inside his shell, or if already inside his shell he will come out of his shell.

  • Click on Turtle Head/Body

    • Clicking the turtle's head or body will turn the turtle into a laser turtle, which will shoot lasers out of his eyes and he'll do a full clockwise turn shooting his lasers.

  • Click anywhere else on canvas

    • Clicking anywhere else on the canvas will make the turtle rotate to that position and then walk towards it.
